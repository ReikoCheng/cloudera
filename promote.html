Cloudera Connect Logo and Guidelines

We encourage you to use the Cloudera Connect logo to promote your partnership with Cloudera. Please refer to the Guidelines below for use requirements. We would appreciate your letting us know where you are using the logo.


The Cloudera and Cloudera Connect Partner Program logos are approved for use in their original form ONLY. Alteration of any Cloudera logos may result in removal and loss of privileges to display. Alteration is hereby defined as the following:


Rebuilding, redrawing or recreating the logo


Cropping, squeezing, stretching, removal of text, manipulation or distortion the logo


Altering field color, typeface, layout or naming convention of the logo


Adding visual graphics to – or around – the logo (See Guidelines above for specifics)


Avoid placing a Cloudera Connect Partner Program logo in the headline, subhead, or body copy of documents or press releases. Please observe the approved logo specifications included in this guide.
