Eligible Marketing Activities

Webinars In-person Seminars Road shows General and industry-specific events Telemarketing Direct marketing (postal mailing) Digital marketing (keyword advertising, emails, SEO campaigns) Customer success stories Case studies Promotional items and giveaways at an approved event
