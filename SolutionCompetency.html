Why Attain A Solution Competency?
Solution Competency recognize the Cloudera Connect partners who have the capabilities and proven customer success that Cloudera customers need
as they transform their data strategies. Cloudera customers can confidently choose business partners based on their specific business needs and industry.

Benefits For Customers

Listing on the Cloudera Partner Listing
The right to use the Cloudera Connect Solution Competency / Industry Competency logo on your website and in your promotional content
Lead routing via an information request form included on the Cloudera Solution Gallery
Exposure to the Cloudera field via a listing on our internal Partner Solutions Directory
Webinars to the Cloudera field (Gold partners and above) and to prospective customers (Platinum partners)
Joint blog posts and press releases on customer successes (approval required)
How Attain A Solution Competency?
Request

Send a request to the Cloudera Connect team by filling out the Solution Competency form.

Document

With your request, provide a solution architecture specific to Cloudera products, with integrations and security details as appropriate.Include two use cases from customers that are live with the solution. For an Industry Competency, these two use cases must be from customers in the same industry.

Review

The Cloudera Connect Solutions Competency team reviews your submission. Be prepared to respond to any clarifying questions on the architecture or solution design to facilitate the approval process.

Complete

Once you have your solution approved, Cloudera will send you an email confirmation with the Solution Competency / Industry Competency logo. At this point, you may provide content for the Cloudera Solution Gallery using the template we provide.

Refresh

Update your solution to take advantage of new Cloudera capabilities and notify us of any changes.
